### **Caverns, Mines & Hidden Area Maps**

This is an optional extension for the Classic & TBC Dungeon Maps patch. It adds extra locations not covered by the main patch: caves, mines, dungeon entrances, starting zones, and hidden areas like GM Island and Hyjal.

* Requires the **Classic & TBC Dungeon Maps** patch to be installed
* Requires the **WDM** addon and **Astrolabe** to be installed and active

**Related files:** `Data\<lang>\patch-<lang>-M.MPQ`, `Data\<lang>\patch-<lang>-N.MPQ`
