### **Saved Accounts, Server List & Custom Login Screens**

This patch adds several quality-of-life improvements to your login experience:

* Create and manage a list of your favorite servers
* Save multiple accounts for quick login (separately for each server)
* Customize the login screen background and music

All settings and saved data are stored in: `Interface\loginui.lua`

**Related files:** `Data\patch-C.MPQ`, `Data\<lang>\patch-<lang>-C.MPQ`, `wow.exe`, `Interface\loginui.lua`
