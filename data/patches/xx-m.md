### **Classic & TBC Dungeon Maps**

This patch restores 56 maps from retail World of Warcraft that are missing in the 3.3.5a client, including Classic and The Burning Crusade dungeons, continents, and battlegrounds.

**Related files:** `Data\<lang>\patch-<lang>-M.MPQ`
